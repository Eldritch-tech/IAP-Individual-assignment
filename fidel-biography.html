Fidel Castro, born on October 25, 2001, in Nairobi, embarked on an educational journey that began at Kabete Vet Lab Primary School and continued through Kanga High School, where he not only excelled academically but also explored diverse interests. Currently pursuing studies at Jommo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology(JKUAT), Fidel has supplemented his academic pursuits with practical skills, attending a driving school and a photography and videography program. Having successfully passed his high school examinations, Fidel is resolutely focused on his career as a developer, contributing to the ever-evolving landscape of technology with a blend of academic excellence and practical expertise. His journey reflects a commitment to continuous learning and a passion for making a meaningful impact in the field of technology.
